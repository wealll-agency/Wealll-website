Our T20 2026's SEO Service Checklist for On-Page #1 SEO Agency Kolkata

Many internet users must pay more attention to the basics of on-page SEO tactics. Digital Piloto's clients in Kolkata might gain from honest and ethical white-hat SEO services.

Include your keyword in the URL of your website

Creating SEO-friendly URLs for On-Page SEO is crucial to improving your website's visibility in the search engine results pages (SERPs). Using the keyword you want to rank for in the URL can help search engines better understand what your page is about.

Use Short URLs for your Webpages (less than 60 characters)

By leveraging shortened URLs, you can optimize your website and make it simpler for users to find and recall your page. Utilizing short URLs will give a significant boost to both the user experience of your site and its visibility.

Use Rich Snippets for Better SEO Rankings

Rich snippets are pieces of code that offer additional information about a web page or content, such as reviews, ratings, product descriptions, contact details, etc. Rich snippets can help search engine robots better understand your website's content.

Place Targeted Keywords in the Title Tag

By including keyword phrases in the title tag for the pages of your website, you can ensure that your content will be easily discovered and indexed by the search engines. Utilizing targeted keywords within the title tags also allows you to prioritize the most important content.

A Short and Crisp Page Title (50-60 Characters)

To optimize SEO, create concise and compelling titles for your pages. Short titles can be more accessible for search engine robots to process and more attractive to potential visitors. Aim for 50-60 character-long titles, which is optimal for SEO purposes.

Incorporating Modifiers to Your Title Tags for Improved CTR

By including words such as 'best,' 'top,' or 'ultimate' at the beginning of your title tags, you can increase engagement and create a more attractive proposition for potential visitors. Modifiers also allow search engine robots to discern the intent of your page faster.

Optimize Graphics & Images on Your Webpages

Enhancing website graphics and images can quickly improve the user experience. High-quality images compressed to smaller files will reduce page load times, provide visitors with an appealing visual, and optimize SEO rankings. Adding relevant captions or keywords to image titles and alt-texts will help search engines.

Work on Heading Tags (H1, H2, H3, H4, H5, H6) Correctly

Heading tags can improve the structure of your website and make it easier for users to find information quickly. By correctly incorporating these tags into your content, you can give each page a hierarchical structure that allows search engine robots to index it appropriately.

Insert Internal Links on Your Webpages

Internal links are a vital part of any website and can help to improve the structure, usability, and SEO website rankings. Visitors can quickly navigate between different web pages by including internal links on each page without searching for them manually.

Optimize Your On-Page SEO by Using LSI Keywords

An effective placement of Latent Semantic Indexing (LSI) keywords into your on-page SEO is essential for improving the visibility of your in SERPs. LSI keywords are words, phrases or terms that fit within the context of a particular topic, allowing search engine algorithms to determine what content is most relevant.

Optimize the Loading Speed of Your Web Page

Web page loading speed is essential to user experience and website optimization. The work performed to optimize the loading speed of your web pages is by reducing file sizes, compressing images, caching files, minifying code, and leveraging browser caching.

Make Sure Your Webpages Are Mobile-Friendly (Responsive Design)

Ensuring that your website is mobile-friendly is salient in today's digital world. With most internet traffic from mobile devices, having a responsive design that works well on all devices is vital to keeping visitors engaged and increasing potential sales.

Check for Broken Links on Your Webpages

Broken links on your web pages can confuse visitors and hurt your website's reputation and search engine rankings. It is vital to regularly check for broken links, both internal and external, to ensure a positive user experience.

Create XML & HTML Sitemaps for Better Website Crawling

XML and HTML sitemaps are vital to any website because they help search engine crawlers discover and index pages. An XML sitemap provides a complete list of all the URLs that are publicly accessible on a website that must be submitted to search engines for faster crawling.

Add Social Sharing Buttons to Your Webpages

Social media sharing buttons are essential for building a more substantial online presence and increasing website traffic. Adding these buttons to your web pages lets visitors quickly share content with their friends, leading to more engagement and new potential customers.

Use Canonical Tags on Similar Pages of Your Website

Canonical tags are essential for informing search engines which page should receive credit when multiple pages on your website have similar content. Canonical tags prevent the dilution of page rank and ensure that search engines index the most relevant version of a page.

Include an Attractive & Compelling Meta Description for Each Page

Meta descriptions are an essential part of your website's SEO strategy. These snippets of text, commonly displayed in search engine results, provide a concise overview of the content on each page.

Keep Your Meta Descriptions Short & Sweet (under 155 characters)

When it comes to meta descriptions, less is more. Short and sweet meta descriptions are essential for SEO success - keep them under 155 characters for optimal results.

Use Outbound Links to Establish Authority

Incorporating outbound links into your content is a great way to improve your website's authority. Outbound links point from one website to another, showing readers that you are knowledgeable about the topic you are discussing.

Insert Schema Markup Code in the Header Section of Your Pages

Using schema markup code is essential in optimizing your website for search engine rankings. By inserting this code in the header section of your pages, you allow search engines to understand your website's content better.
